The Problem

DeFi lacks privacy by design. Every trade is transparent, exploitable, and tracked.
  • MEV Extraction: $1B+ extracted from traders annually through front-running and sandwich attacks
  • Privacy Crisis: All transactions are public—whales can't move without market impact
  • Fragmented Liquidity: Cross-chain swaps require multiple bridges, exposing users to exploits
  • Trust Barriers: Institutions won't adopt DeFi without transaction privacy

Current DEXs optimize for speed and liquidity, but privacy and security are afterthoughts.

How It Works

Privacy Flow Engine

  • User submits trade intent (not public transaction)
  • CoW Protocol batches intents into dark auctions
  • Solvers compete to find best execution
  • Settlement happens on-chain—zero front-running
  • Optional privacy quests for enhanced anonymity

DLMM Pools

  • Liquidity concentrated in active price bins
  • LPs earn higher fees per dollar deployed
  • Auto-rebalancing based on volatility
  • Lower slippage for large trades

Cross-Chain Security

  • CCIP for general message passing & token transfers
  • CCTP for native USDC swaps (no wrapped tokens)
  • Unified UX across Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Ethereum, Avalanche
