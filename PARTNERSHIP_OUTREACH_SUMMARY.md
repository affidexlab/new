## ❓ FAQ - Questions You Might Have

### Q: Should I send all DMs from DecaFlow's main account or my personal account?

**A:** Use DecaFlow's official account if it has decent following (500+) and looks professional. Otherwise, use your personal account with clear founder/team title in bio.

---

### Q: What if they ask about funding/investment opportunities?

**A:** Be honest about bootstrapped status. Frame it as:
*"We're currently bootstrapped which allows us to move fast and be flexible with partnerships. Happy to discuss strategic opportunities as we grow."*

---

### Q: How do I handle if they want upfront payment for partnership?

**A:** Red flag if they want money just to talk. Green flag if they have standard partnership tiers (common for influencer networks like SquadX). 

Ask for their partnership deck/pricing before committing.

---

### Q: What if multiple projects respond at once?

**A:** Great problem to have! Prioritize based on:
1. Fastest time to value (which can you implement quickest)
2. Largest potential user impact
3. Strategic importance (e.g., SquadX for user acquisition)

---

### Q: Should I offer token allocations or revenue share in initial DMs?

**A:** No. Keep first DM focused on value exchange and use cases. Discuss commercial terms only after mutual interest is established.

## 📞 Need Help During Outreach?

If you hit snags during outreach, here are troubleshooting tips:

**Issue:** Can't verify if Twitter handle is real (lots of impersonators)
**Solution:** Cross-reference with their official website's social links

**Issue:** DM not delivering (grayed out)
**Solution:** They may have DMs restricted to followers. Follow them and try again, or use backup method.

**Issue:** Got a response but not sure how to proceed
**Solution:** Always schedule a call. Don't try to negotiate partnerships entirely over DM.

**Issue:** They're interested but asking for things you don't have (audit, big metrics, etc.)
**Solution:** Be transparent about current stage. Offer pilot/trial partnership with clear success metrics.
