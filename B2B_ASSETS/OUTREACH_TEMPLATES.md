## 💡 Outreach Best Practices

1. **Personalize Every Message**: Research the protocol/person first
2. **Lead with Value**: Show them what they're losing/gaining
3. **Be Specific**: Use actual data from your dashboard
4. **Make it Easy**: Include calendar links, not "let me know when you're free"
5. **Follow Up**: 80% of conversions come after 3+ touchpoints
6. **Don't Be Pushy**: Respect "no" and move on
7. **Track Everything**: Use a CRM or spreadsheet to track responses
