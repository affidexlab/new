## 💡 Outreach Tips

1. **Personalize Every Message**: Mention specific data about THEIR protocol
2. **Lead with Data**: "Your users lost $XXk last month"
3. **Make it Easy**: Include calendar link, not "let me know when you're free"
4. **Follow Up**: 3 touchpoints minimum (Day 0, Day 3, Day 7)
5. **Be Helpful**: Offer value before asking for anything
6. **Stay Organized**: Use CRM or spreadsheet
7. **Track Everything**: Response rates, objections, timing
