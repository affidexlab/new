## 🎯 WHEN TO MENTION WHAT

### **In First DM:**
✅ Their specific metrics (show you did research)  
✅ Your core value prop (1 sentence)  
✅ Clear mutual benefit  
✅ Soft CTA (call or more info)

### **In Follow-Up DM:**
✅ Reference their recent activity  
✅ Add urgency (Q1 2025 plans)  
✅ Make it easy to respond (yes/no question)

### **On First Call:**
✅ Ask about their priorities (listen 60% of time)  
✅ Share specific partnership model  
✅ Provide examples of success metrics  
✅ Agree on next steps with timeline

### **DON'T Mention (Until Later):**
❌ Your funding situation (unless asked)  
❌ Specific revenue share terms (too early)  
❌ Token allocations (compliance issues)  
❌ Your other partnerships in progress (maintain focus)
