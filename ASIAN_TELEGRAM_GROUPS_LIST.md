## 📝 JOINING PROTOCOL

### DO:
1. **Read the Rules** - Every group has rules. Read them first!
2. **Observe First** - Lurk for 1-2 hours before posting
3. **Introduce Politely** - Use templates from Quick Start Guide
4. **Engage Authentically** - Answer questions, be helpful
5. **Customize Per Group** - Adjust message to country/community
6. **Respond Quickly** - Reply to all comments on your posts

### DON'T:
1. **Spam** - Don't post in multiple groups within minutes
2. **Copy-Paste Blindly** - Customize each message
3. **Ignore Responses** - Always engage with replies
4. **Post Off-Topic** - Stay relevant to group's focus
5. **Be Pushy** - Soft introduction, not hard sell
6. **Argue** - Stay professional even if criticized
7. **Post at Wrong Times** - Respect time zones

## 🚦 RED FLAGS - GROUPS TO AVOID

### Skip Groups That:
- Have 0-50 members (too small)
- No activity in 7+ days (dead group)
- Only admin posts allowed
- All messages are spam (no real discussion)
- "Pump and dump" focus
- Scam warnings in description
- Require payment to join
- "Signal" or "free signals" groups (usually scams)

---

## ✅ GREEN FLAGS - GOOD GROUPS

### Look For:
- 500+ active members
- Daily discussions (multiple per hour)
- Helpful community (people answer questions)
- Admins are responsive
- Mix of news and trading discussion
- New project showcases allowed
- Rules are clear
- No spam in recent messages
- Diverse topics (not just one coin)
- Professional/friendly tone
