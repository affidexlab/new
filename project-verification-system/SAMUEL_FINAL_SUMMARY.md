## ✅ THE TWO MAIN VERIFICATION RULES

### Rule 1: Age Check
- ✅ Age must be 18-35 (inclusive)
- ❌ Below 18 or above 35 = NOT ELIGIBLE

### Rule 2: NIN Check
- ✅ Must have BOTH NIN number AND NIN picture
- ❌ Missing either = NOT ELIGIBLE

### Bonus: Passport Check
- ⚠️ Flag if passport photo is a selfie (doesn't disqualify, just flag it)
