### For Each Row (100 down to 2):

1. **Check Age (Column F)**

   - ❌ FAIL: Age field is empty

2. **Check NIN Number (Column P)**

   - ❌ FAIL: Empty or incomplete

3. **Check NIN Picture (Column O)**

4. **Check Passport Picture (Column Q)**
