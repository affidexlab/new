## 🚨 RED FLAGS - DON'T PROCEED IF:

❌ They ask for upfront payment to "discuss partnership"  
❌ They want access to your smart contracts/private keys  
❌ They're not verified (check website matches Twitter)  
❌ No social media activity in 30+ days  
❌ Website permanently down (7+ days)

---

## ✅ GREEN LIGHTS - GOOD SIGNS:

✅ They respond within 48 hours  
✅ They ask specific questions about integration  
✅ They offer to schedule a call  
✅ They mention other partners they've worked with  
✅ They share their partnership process/deck

## 🎯 SUCCESS DEFINITION

**You're doing great if:**
- 2+ projects respond within 1 week (40% response rate)
- 1+ call scheduled within 2 weeks
- 1 partnership closed within 4-6 weeks

**Don't stress if:**
- Some don't respond (totally normal)
- You need to send follow-ups
- Conversations take 2-3 weeks to develop

**Partnerships take time. Stay consistent.**

---

## 🆘 NEED HELP?

**If you're stuck:**

1. **No responses after 10 days?**
   - Try backup contact methods (see quick_contact_reference.md)
   - Expand to 5-10 more projects
   - Review DMs for improvements

2. **Getting responses but they fizzle out?**
   - Respond faster (within 12 hours ideal)
   - Offer clear next step (call)
   - Don't try to negotiate over DM

3. **They're asking for things you don't have?**
   - Be transparent about current stage
   - Offer pilot/trial partnership
   - Focus on mutual learning
