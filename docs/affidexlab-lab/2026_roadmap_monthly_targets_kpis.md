# Affidex Lab — 2026 Roadmap (Monthly Targets + Actions + KPIs)

North Star for 2026
- Make Affidex Lab profitable through cashflow-first studio operations (productized sprints + retainers + sponsorships), then raise funding from strength.

Core KPI categories (track monthly)
- Revenue: cash collected, MRR, gross margin %, net margin %
- Sales: new leads added, outreaches sent, calls booked, proposals sent, close rate, avg deal size
- Delivery: on-time delivery %, client satisfaction/NPS, # case studies published
- Funding: grants submitted, grants approved $, sponsor $ closed, investor meetings
- Brand: inbound qualified leads, newsletter subs, partner count

Baseline assumptions
- Team strength: smart contract deployment, basic audit/readiness, growth/BD.
- Capacity: up to 15 concurrent workstreams if demand exists (requires standardized roles and QA gates).
