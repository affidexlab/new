## Key Messaging Principles Used in DMs:

✅ **Specificity:** Mention their actual stats, features, recent achievements  
✅ **Mutual Benefit:** Always frame as "win-win"  
✅ **Clear Value Prop:** What they get, what you get  
✅ **Urgency (soft):** Timeline mentions without being pushy  
✅ **Professionalism + Personality:** Emojis for Web3 culture, but serious intent  
✅ **Call-to-Action:** Always end with clear next step  
✅ **Brevity:** Keep under 200 words when possible (people skim DMs)
