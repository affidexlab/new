## CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### If Things Go Wrong

**Scenario 1: Very Low User Activity**
- Don't panic - typical for new DEX
- Double incentives for first 100 users
- Personal outreach to every wallet that tried product
- Free 1-on-1 onboarding calls
- Simplify UX further

**Scenario 2: Technical Issues**
- Communicate immediately and transparently
- Pause if necessary (better than bad experience)
- Compensate affected users (bonus points/refunds)
- Post-mortem analysis
- Implement fixes ASAP

**Scenario 3: No Partnership Interest**
- Lower your ask (start with simple integrations)
- Offer more value (we'll promote you more than you promote us)
- Build more traction first
- Focus on community growth instead

**Scenario 4: Running Out of Money**
- Cut paid marketing
- Focus on organic only
- Raise angel round ($50K-$100K from friends/family)
- Apply for grants
- Consider revenue generation (increase fees temporarily)

**Scenario 5: Competitor Launches**
- Don't change strategy mid-flight
- Study their approach
- Differentiate more clearly
- Focus on YOUR strengths
- Community loyalty matters more than features

## FINAL THOUGHTS

### You're Not Too Late
- Crypto moves fast. 4 days behind is nothing.
- With focused execution, you can catch up in 2 weeks.
- The team that executes fastest wins, not the team that launches first.

### Focus is Key
- Don't try to do everything in this plan
- Pick the tactics that match your strengths
- 80/20 rule: 20% of efforts = 80% of results
- For you, that's probably: Points + Community + Twitter

### Consistency Beats Intensity
- Better to post 2 tweets every day than 20 tweets one day
- Better to engage daily than big push once a week
- Show up. Every. Single. Day.

### Community is Everything
- Users become community
