# DecaFlow — Private Liquidity Layer (Base Native)

DecaFlow is a privacy-first DeFi trading and liquidity protocol built on **Base**, designed for traders who want **MEV protection**, **better execution**, and **cross-chain access** without juggling multiple apps.

## What DecaFlow does
- **Privacy swaps / MEV protection:** intent-style execution and private routing to reduce sandwich attacks and front‑running.
- **DLMM liquidity pools:** denser liquidity for improved capital efficiency and stronger LP fee capture.
- **Cross-chain swaps & bridging:** secure rails across major chains (with smart routing for best routes and cost).

## Why it matters
DeFi is transparent by default. That makes large and small trades easy to exploit. DecaFlow focuses on **quiet execution**, **lower slippage**, and a **clean user experience** for multi‑chain traders.
