# VDM Staking Guide - Graphics Design Prompt

## Project Overview
Create a professional, multi-slide infographic guide for VDM token staking on the DecaFlow platform. The guide should be visually engaging, easy to understand, and suitable for social media sharing and educational purposes.

---

## Brand Guidelines

### Color Palette
**Primary Colors**:
- DecaFlow Blue: `#3396FF` (main brand color)
- DecaFlow Light Blue: `#47A1FF` (accents)
- VDM Colors: Use VDM's official brand colors where appropriate
- Dark Background: `#0A0E27` to `#1a1f3a` (gradient)

**Supporting Colors**:
- Success Green: `#10B981` (for positive actions, rewards)
- Warning Orange: `#F59E0B` (for fees, important notes)
- Text White: `#FFFFFF` (primary text)
- Text Gray: `#94A3B8` (secondary text)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter Bold, SF Pro Display Bold)
- **Body Text**: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter Regular, SF Pro Text)
- **Numbers/Stats**: Medium to Bold weight for emphasis

### Visual Style
- Modern, professional fintech aesthetic
- Clean layouts with generous white space
- Use gradient backgrounds (dark blues to purples)
- Rounded corners on cards and buttons
- Subtle shadows for depth
- Icons: Line-style or minimal filled icons

## Slide 9: FAQ Section (Part 1)
**Title**: "Frequently Asked Questions"

**Visual Layout**: Q&A cards with alternating colors

### Q1: What happens if I disconnect my wallet?
**A**: Your stake is secure on the blockchain. You can reconnect anytime to view your position and claim when unlocked.

### Q2: Can I add more VDM to my existing stake?
**A**: No, each wallet can only have one active stake. You must wait for your current stake to complete and claim before staking again.

### Q3: How are rewards calculated?
**A**: Rewards are calculated based on your net staked amount (after deposit fee) multiplied by the APY for your chosen lock period.

### Q4: When can I claim my rewards?
**A**: You can claim your principal (VDM) and rewards (USDT) once your lock period has ended. A countdown timer on your dashboard shows remaining time.

**Design**: Use speech bubble or card design for each Q&A pair

## Slide 10: FAQ Section (Part 2)
**Title**: "More Frequently Asked Questions"

### Q5: Is my VDM safe during the staking period?
**A**: Yes. Your VDM is held in a secure custodial wallet managed by Affidex Lab and VDM team. The staking system is audited and monitored regularly.

### Q6: What wallets are supported?
**A**: Any Solana-compatible wallet including Phantom, Solflare, Sollet, and others. Connect at decaflow.xyz/staking.

### Q7: Are there any hidden fees?
**A**: No. Only two fees: 2.5% deposit fee and 1.5% withdrawal fee. These are clearly displayed before you confirm your stake.

### Q8: What is the minimum staking amount?
**A**: The minimum stake is 1,000 VDM tokens. There is no maximum limit.

## Slide 11: FAQ Section (Part 3)
**Title**: "Technical & Security Questions"

### Q9: How do I get VDM tokens to stake?
**A**: You can purchase VDM tokens on supported exchanges and transfer them to your Solana wallet before staking.

### Q10: What network is VDM staking on?
**A**: VDM staking operates on the Solana blockchain. Ensure your wallet is connected to Solana mainnet.

### Q11: Can I unstake early in case of emergency?
**A**: No early unstaking is permitted. Your tokens are locked for the full duration you selected. Plan accordingly.

### Q12: How long does it take to claim after unlocking?
**A**: Once your lock period ends, you submit a claim request. Payouts are processed by the Affidex/VDM operations team, typically within 24-48 hours.

## Design Specifications

### Format Options:
1. **Instagram Carousel**: 1080x1080px per slide (13 slides)
2. **Twitter/X Thread**: 1200x675px per slide (landscape)
3. **LinkedIn Post**: 1200x627px per slide
4. **PDF Guide**: 8.5x11" or A4 size
5. **Website Embeddable**: Responsive design

### Export Formats:
- PNG (high resolution, 300 DPI for print)
- PDF (for comprehensive guide download)
- SVG (for web use)
- MP4 (if creating animated version)

### Accessibility:
- Ensure text has sufficient contrast (WCAG AA minimum)
- Use alt text descriptions for all icons
- Font size minimum 14px for body text
- Headlines minimum 24px

### Branding:
- Include VDM logo on every slide (top corner)
- Include DecaFlow logo on every slide (opposite corner)
- Consistent footer with "decaflow.xyz/staking" on each slide
- Use slide numbers (e.g., "1/13", "2/13", etc.)

## Additional Notes for Designer

### Tone & Style:
- Professional but approachable
- Educational, not salesy
- Build trust through clarity and transparency
- Emphasize security and partnership

### Visual Hierarchy:
- Most important information (APY rates, key steps) should be largest
- Use color to guide attention (green for benefits, orange for cautions)
- Consistent spacing and alignment across all slides

### Icons & Illustrations:
- Use consistent icon style throughout (all line icons OR all filled)
- Consider custom illustrations for key concepts (staking, rewards, claiming)
- Avoid generic stock photos; use abstract shapes or crypto-themed graphics

### Animation Suggestions (Optional):
- Slide 4 (How It Works): Animate steps appearing in sequence
- Slide 3 (APY cards): Cards flip or slide in
- Slide 12 (CTA): Pulsing "Stake Now" button
- Slide 8 (Calculator): Numbers count up animation

### Testing:
- Ensure legibility on mobile screens (most users will view on phones)
- Test color contrast for colorblind accessibility
- Preview on dark mode if applicable
- Check that all text is readable at small sizes
