# DecaFlow ▸ Tychi Wallet
## Mutual Non-Disclosure & Integration Licence Agreement (Draft)

**Effective Date:** ____________  
**Parties:**  
- **DecaFlow Protocol** (“DecaFlow”), a decentralized software publisher operated by Affidex Labs, with principal business operations in San Francisco, CA.  
- **Tychi Wallet / Tychi Limited** (“Tychi”), a multi-chain wallet platform operated under Tychi Limited.

DecaFlow and Tychi are referred to collectively as the “Parties” or individually as a “Party.”

---

## 1. Purpose
The Parties intend to evaluate and implement a strategic integration whereby DecaFlow provides decentralized exchange, liquidity management, and cross-chain bridge infrastructure inside Tychi’s wallet applications (“Integration”). In connection with this Integration, each Party may disclose Confidential Information and grant usage rights to proprietary software components. This Agreement governs confidentiality obligations and the limited licence necessary to complete the Integration.

---

## 2. Definitions
1. **Confidential Information** means all non-public information disclosed by a Party, whether oral, written, electronic, or visual, that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential, including but not limited to product roadmaps, source code, smart-contract ABIs, deployment details, API keys, pricing, user metrics, legal terms, and business strategies. Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available without breach of this Agreement; (b) was known to the receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) was independently developed without use of the disclosing Party’s Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without confidentiality obligation.
2. **Integration Assets** mean DecaFlow APIs, SDKs, smart contracts, documentation, and associated materials provided for the purpose of embedding DecaFlow services inside Tychi Wallet.
3. **Licence Term** means the duration set forth in Section 9 of this Agreement.

---

## 3. Confidentiality Obligations
1. Each Party will use the disclosing Party’s Confidential Information solely for evaluating, integrating, operating, or supporting the Integration.  
2. Confidential Information shall be protected using at least the same degree of care the receiving Party uses to protect its own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care.  
3. Confidential Information may be shared only with employees, contractors, or advisors who have a legitimate need to know and are bound by written confidentiality obligations no less protective than those herein.  
4. The receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information when required by law, provided it gives prompt written notice (unless legally prohibited) to allow the disclosing Party to seek protective orders.

---

## 4. Integration Licence
1. **Grant to Tychi.** DecaFlow grants Tychi a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable licence during the Licence Term to access and embed the Integration Assets within Tychi’s wallet interfaces (mobile, desktop, browser extension) solely for the Integration. This licence permits Tychi to call DecaFlow APIs, include DecaFlow SDK components, and display DecaFlow branding as mutually agreed.  
2. **Restrictions.** Tychi shall not: (a) resell or sublicense the Integration Assets to third parties; (b) decompile, reverse engineer, or create derivative works of DecaFlow’s proprietary code (except to the extent permitted by law); (c) use the Integration Assets to build a competing routing engine; or (d) remove or obscure proprietary notices.  
3. **Ownership.** DecaFlow retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Integration Assets, including improvements. Tychi retains ownership of its wallet applications, user interfaces, and customer relationships. No joint ownership is created by this Agreement.  
4. **Feedback.** Tychi may provide suggestions or improvements regarding the Integration. DecaFlow may use such feedback without restriction and without obligation.

---

## 5. Security & API Usage
1. Tychi will maintain industry-standard security controls to protect API keys, SDKs, and smart-contract interactions and will immediately notify DecaFlow of any suspected compromise.  
2. DecaFlow may monitor API usage for performance, quota management, and security purposes. DecaFlow reserves the right to throttle or suspend API access if Tychi violates usage limits or security requirements, following reasonable notice.  
3. Both Parties will follow mutually agreed incident response procedures, including 24/7 escalation contacts supplied in the Partner Integration Kit.

---

## 6. Compliance & User Obligations
1. Each Party is responsible for complying with applicable laws, including AML/CFT requirements. DecaFlow operates non-custodial infrastructure and does not take possession of user funds. Tychi remains responsible for end-user onboarding, KYC (if any), and regulatory obligations specific to its jurisdictions.  
2. Neither Party will knowingly use the Integration Assets to facilitate sanctioned activities or jurisdictions. If a regulator requests information, the receiving Party shall—where legally permitted—promptly notify the other Party.

---

## 7. Marketing & Branding
1. Any public announcements or marketing materials referencing the Integration must be pre-approved in writing by both Parties, except for statements previously cleared or required by law.  
2. Tychi may display “Powered by DecaFlow” or comparable branding in swap, liquidity, and bridge interfaces, consistent with DecaFlow’s brand guidelines.  
3. DecaFlow may list Tychi as an official wallet partner on its website and marketing channels.

---

## 8. Fees & Revenue Sharing (Optional)
If the Parties agree to revenue sharing, referral fees, or other commercial terms, such terms will be detailed in a separate addendum referencing this Agreement. Absent such addendum, each Party bears its own costs and no revenue sharing is implied.

---

## 9. Term & Termination
1. **Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for two (2) years (“Licence Term”), automatically renewing for additional one-year periods unless either Party provides 30 days’ written notice prior to renewal.  
2. **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days’ written notice.  
3. **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within 15 days of notice.  
4. **Post-Termination.** Upon termination, Tychi will cease using the Integration Assets, revoke API keys from production systems, and delete or return all DecaFlow Confidential Information, except as required for legal retention. Sections 2–12 survive termination.
