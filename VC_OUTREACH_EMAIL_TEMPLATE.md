## Email Best Practices

### Do's:
✅ Keep it under 150 words (busy VCs skim)
✅ Lead with traction (numbers > vision)
✅ Make the subject line specific (not generic)
✅ Include clear CTA (15-min call, not "let's chat")
✅ Attach deck as PDF (not Google Drive link)
✅ Follow up 3-5 days later if no response
✅ Personalize every email (mention their portfolio, thesis, tweets)

### Don'ts:
❌ Don't send wall of text
❌ Don't oversell ("revolutionize DeFi")
❌ Don't hide the ask (be direct about raise)
❌ Don't attach huge files (keep deck under 5MB)
❌ Don't CC multiple VCs on same email
❌ Don't send on weekends (send Tue-Thu mornings)
