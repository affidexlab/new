### Alternative Strategies:

1. **Email Outreach:**
   - Look for "partnerships@", "business@", "hello@" on their websites
   - Use Hunter.io or similar tools to find team emails
   
2. **Community Channels:**
   - Join their Discord/Telegram communities
   - Engage for 3-5 days, then ask for partnership contact
   
3. **Mutual Connections:**
   - Check LinkedIn for mutual connections
   - Ask for warm intro
   
4. **Event-Based Outreach:**
   - If they're attending conferences, meet in person
   - Sponsor/attend same virtual events
   
5. **Content Collaboration:**
   - Propose guest blog post or Twitter Space first
   - Build relationship before formal partnership ask

## Red Flags to Watch For

⚠️ **Don't proceed if:**
- They ask for upfront payment to "discuss partnership"
- They want access to your smart contracts/private keys
- They're not verified on Twitter (check for impersonators)
- Website has been down for 7+ days (may be defunct)
- No social media activity in 30+ days

✅ **Green lights:**
- Active social media (posting regularly)
- Verified website with working product
- Transparent team information
- Recent partnership announcements
- Engaged community

## Best Practices for DM Outreach

### DO:
✅ Personalize each message (no copy-paste obvious templates)  
✅ Keep first DM under 150 words  
✅ Include specific data about their project (shows you did research)  
✅ Lead with value for them, not you  
✅ Have a clear call-to-action  
✅ Follow up if no response in 72 hours  
✅ Be professional but match their communication style  

### DON'T:
❌ Send generic "let's partner" messages  
❌ Oversell DecaFlow in first message  
❌ Use aggressive sales language  
❌ Send multiple DMs in one day  
❌ Tag them publicly if they don't respond  
❌ Offer equity/tokens in first message (compliance issues)  
❌ Make demands or set arbitrary deadlines
